Search tips
  • "motor vehicle" — exact phrase
  • agency:nypd — only NYPD datasets
  • tag:permits — datasets tagged "permits"
  • type:map — only maps (or chart, file, dataset)
  • cat:"public safety" — limit to a category
  • updated:30d — updated in last 30 days (or 1y, old)
  • -tag:historical — exclude a tag
